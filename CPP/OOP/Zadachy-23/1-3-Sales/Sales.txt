Write a class Sale holding the following data: town, product, price, quantity. Read a list of sales and calculate and
print the total sales by the town as shown in the output. Order the towns alphabetically in the output.

Input :
5
Sofia beer 1.20 160
Varna chocolate 2.35 86
Sofia coffee 0.40 853
Varna apple 0.86 75.44
Plovdiv beer 1.10 88

Ouput:
Plovdiv -> 1.10 * 88 = 96.80
Sofia -> 1.20 * 160 + 0.40 * 853 = 533.20
Varna -> 2.35 * 86 + 0.86 * 75.44 = 266.98
